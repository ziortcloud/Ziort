ZiMatch — Complete Journey

to Payment

Every step from posting a need to completing it and earning commission.

The 8-Step Lifecycle
01
Requirement Posted
Requester (ZiOrbit user or public guest) fills a simple form: What do you need? Quantity? Location? Budget? Deadline? Takes 60 seconds. No account needed for guests — just phone number for OTP.
ZiOrbit users get auto-filled details from their app (ZiRetail stock level, ZiCare inventory etc.)
02
ZiMatch Categorizes & Routes
Platform reads the requirement, auto-assigns category (supply/service/transport/labour/professional), determines relevant suppliers in the requester's city and neighbouring areas.
Verified suppliers in matching category get instant push notification + WhatsApp via ZiPost
03
Suppliers Submit Proposals
Verified suppliers (priority listed, shown first) and unverified applicants both submit proposals. Proposal includes: price, delivery timeline, terms, supplier rating, past completion count.
Unverified suppliers can apply but shown below verified ones — incentive to get verified and pay listing fee
04
Requester Reviews & Selects
Requester sees all proposals ranked by: verified status, rating, price, proximity. Can message suppliers directly within ZiMatch. Selects the best proposal — one tap to confirm.
ZiPartner agents can assist requesters in selecting — earn referral commission if they close the deal
05
Deal Locked — Escrow Initiated
Once selected, ZiPay holds payment in escrow. Supplier sees confirmed order. Requester's funds are secured. Neither party can back out without penalty. Work officially begins.
For large orders: 50% upfront escrow, 50% on delivery. Configurable per category.
06
Work / Delivery in Progress
Supplier fulfills the requirement. If transport involved → ZiFleet/ZiPilot handles delivery with live tracking. Both parties get ZiPost updates at key milestones: started, in transit, arriving.
Status: In Progress. Requester can raise a dispute at any point. ZiOrbit mediates.
07
Requester Confirms Completion
Requester marks requirement as fulfilled (or auto-confirmed after 48hrs with no dispute). Both parties leave ratings. The requirement becomes a completed case study visible on supplier's profile.
Auto-confirm after 48 hrs protects supplier from being held hostage by slow requesters
08
Payment Released — Everyone Earns
ZiPay releases escrow. Platform deducts commission automatically. Net amount credited to supplier's ZiPay wallet instantly. ZiPartner agent commission credited if applicable. ZiLedger records the transaction for both parties.
Supplier receives: deal amount − ZiOrbit commission (3–8% based on category)
ZiOrbit receives: commission + supplier's monthly rental (pre-collected)
Agent receives: 1–2% referral if they assisted

ZiMatch — Business Model

Three Revenue
Streams

Commission + listing rental + priority boost. All automated. All compounding.

① Transaction Commission
3–12% per deal
Taken automatically from every completed deal via ZiPay escrow. Rate varies by category — supply 3–5%, transport 5–8%, professional services 8–12%. Guest suppliers pay higher commission — incentive to upgrade to paid listing tier. Commission is locked at deal time, cannot be disputed. This is the primary revenue engine.
② Supplier Listing Rental
₹299–₹1,999/month
Monthly subscription paid by suppliers to be listed and verified on ZiMatch. This is recurring revenue that doesn't depend on deal volume. Even if a supplier has a slow month with no deals, their listing fee still pays. Target: 500 suppliers at avg ₹600/month = ₹3L/month from listing fees alone, regardless of transaction volume.
③ Priority Boost (Ad-Free Ranking)
₹99–₹499 per boost
Supplier pays to appear at the top of a specific requirement category for 7 or 30 days. Not traditional ads — pure ranking boost within relevant requirements. A rice distributor pays ₹199 to be top-ranked for all "rice/grains" requirements in Hyderabad for 30 days. Highly targeted, measurable ROI for supplier.
Revenue Projection at Scale
── SCENARIO: 200 suppliers, 100 deals/month avg ────── Listing Rentals (recurring, deal-independent): 50 Basic × ₹299 = ₹14,950 100 Pro × ₹799 = ₹79,900 50 Featured × ₹1,999 = ₹99,950 Total listing: ₹1,94,800/month Transaction Commission (deal volume): 100 deals × avg deal ₹25,000 × avg 6% comm = ₹1,50,000/month Priority Boosts: 50 boosts × avg ₹250 = ₹12,500/month ───────────────────────────────────────────── ZiMatch alone: ₹3.57L/month at 200 suppliers At 2,000 suppliers + 1,000 deals: ₹35L+/month ───────────────────────────────────────────── This is ON TOP of all Zi-app subscription revenue And compounds — more suppliers = more deals = more commission

ZiMatch — Implementation

How to Build
ZiMatch

Exact sequence. Start lean, grow into the full marketplace.

Phase 1 — Now
MVP: Post requirement → notify suppliers → deal done
Build zi_match_requirements table + simple post form. Guest posts via OTP phone verification.
Build zi_match_suppliers table. Manually onboard your first 10 suppliers in Hyderabad across 2–3 categories.
When requirement posted → ZiPost sends WhatsApp to matching suppliers. Simple text message: "New requirement: [title], [city], ₹[budget]. Reply to apply."
Supplier submits proposal via a simple form (no complex UI needed at MVP). Requester picks one.
Manually facilitate first 20 deals. You are the marketplace engine initially. Learn what goes wrong before automating.
Collect commission manually (UPI transfer + ZiLedger entry) until ZiPay escrow is built.
Phase 1 — Now
Connect ZiRetail + ZiPawn to auto-post requirements
ZiRetail stock.low event → auto-creates requirement on ZiMatch. This is the killer feature — SMB doesn't even need to visit ZiMatch, it just works.
ZiPawn unclaimed item event → auto-creates auction requirement on ZiMatch.
These two connections immediately make ZiMatch useful to your existing ZiOrbit users from day one.
Phase 2 — After 50 Deals Done
ZiPay escrow + supplier dashboard + ratings
Build ZiPay escrow flow — requester pays upfront, funds held, released on confirmation. This removes trust barrier completely.
Supplier dashboard — see incoming requirements, manage proposals, track earnings, view ratings.
Ratings system — both sides rate after completion. Ratings visible on profiles. This self-polices quality.
Introduce supplier listing tiers. Charge Basic (₹299) and Pro (₹799) listing fees. First real recurring revenue from ZiMatch.
Phase 3 — Scale
Full marketplace — all categories, all cities, all features
Expand to all 6 categories. Transport + Labour + Professional services open up ZiFleet, ZiPilot, ZiCare connections.
Priority boost (paid ranking) feature for suppliers. First ad-like revenue stream.
ZiPartner agents get their own ZiMatch dashboard — see open requirements, introduce suppliers, track commission.
Dispute resolution flow — formal mediation process, ZiOrbit as neutral party.
Expand city by city — start Hyderabad, then Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi.
