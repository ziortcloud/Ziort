#1
ZiPartner BUILD FIRST
Agent/reseller network — local partners who onboard SMBs onto ZiOrbit products
Distribution Engine Commission Model PRD Complete
Why #1 — this is your multiplier
You cannot scale ZiPawn door-to-door alone. ZiPartner recruits local agents — accountants, CA students, IT freelancers — who earn commission for every SMB they onboard. One agent can onboard 20 branches. 50 agents = 1,000 branches. No ZiPartner = growth capped at your personal bandwidth.
Build effort: Medium (PRD done)
Revenue model: Agent earns % of subscription
Dependency: None — build immediately
↓ ZiPartner agents then sell these ↓
#2
ZiPost BUILD SECOND
Automated business communication — WhatsApp/SMS notifications, reminders, promotions for SMBs
Credit Pack Revenue High Repeat Usage In Development
Why #2 — recurring revenue layer
Every SMB needs to message customers. ZiPost is a prepaid credit model — SMBs buy SMS/WhatsApp packs and burn through them monthly. This creates predictable recurring revenue on top of subscriptions. Works for pawn brokers (loan due reminders), retailers, clinics, everyone. MSG91 backend already chosen.
Build effort: Medium
Revenue model: Prepaid credit packs
Dependency: MSG91 + TRAI DLT compliance

The Strategic Logic Behind This Order

01
Distribution before product. ZiPartner (#1) unlocks every other product. Without agents, you're selling door-to-door forever. With 50 agents, you have 50 salespeople at zero salary cost.
02
Recurring revenue before verticals. ZiPost credit packs create monthly income that doesn't depend on new signups. ZiLedger subscriptions compound. Both fund the harder builds.
03
One vertical deeply > many verticals shallowly. ZiPawn in 500 branches is more valuable than ZiPawn + ZiRetail + ZiCare each in 50 branches. Depth first, breadth second.
04
Free tools are permanent infrastructure. ZiCalc, ZiInvoice, ZiScan — never monetize these. They are your search presence, your trust signal, and your top-of-funnel forever.
05
Platform plays come last. ZiPay and ZiStore are powerful but complex. Build them when you have the customer base, compliance bandwidth, and capital to do them properly.
