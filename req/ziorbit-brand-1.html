Brand Voice & Tone

Confident

Not "we try to help SMBs." — "Your business, in orbit." Direct. No fluff.

Intelligent

Speaks to business owners as equals. Explains complexity simply, without being condescending.

Global yet grounded

Built in India. Proud of it. Designed for Bharat and beyond — both truths coexist.

Expansive

Orbit = limitless. Always signal growth, not limitation. "More verticals coming." "Your orbit expands."

Usage Rules
  • ✓ Always write ZiOrbit — capital Z, capital O. Never "Ziorbit" or "ZIORBIT".
  • ✓ Product names: ZiPawn, ZiLedger — capital Z, capital first letter of word.
  • ✓ Orbit Cyan (#00d4ff) is the glow/accent. Never use it as a background.
  • ✓ Satellite Gold (#f5a623) is for CTA buttons and price highlights only.
  • ✗ Never stretch, rotate or recolor the hexagon logo.
  • ✗ Never use the wordmark without minimum clear space (equal to height of "Z" on all sides).
  • ✗ Never place the light logo on a light background.

01 — Primary Logo Icon
Icon / Device Mark
A modern tech company logo icon for "ZiOrbit". Bold geometric hexagon shape as the base. Inside the hexagon, a large bold capital letter "Z" in clean sans-serif font, colored in white to electric cyan gradient. Around the Z, a tilted elliptical orbit ring in electric blue (#2d3bff) to cyan (#00d4ff) gradient, with a small glowing satellite dot at the top-right of the orbit ring in white with a cyan glow. The hexagon background is deep dark navy blue (#101862). Overall aesthetic: space-tech, premium SaaS, clean and modern. Flat vector style, no textures, no gradients on background. Symmetrical composition. Suitable for app icon, favicon, and trademark device mark. White padding around the hexagon. Style: professional tech logo, vector illustration, flat design.
💡 Tip: Generate 4 variations. Pick the one where the Z is most legible at small size. The satellite dot should be visible but subtle.

05 — ZiPawn Sub-brand Icon (Sample)
Product Sub-brand
A product icon for "ZiPawn", a digital pawn broker management app under the ZiOrbit platform. Hexagonal icon in deep navy (#101862) with a thin cyan border. Inside the hexagon: the letters "Zi" in small bold white text in the upper-left, and a simple minimalist pawn chess piece silhouette in white in the center, slightly overlapping a faint orbit ring.
